Biography

Toronto native “Michael Ruberu”, is an exotic product of striking melody, inspiring lyrics, and an undisputably phenomenal voice that deserves your ear. With training in Traditional Negro Spirituals and Classical Voice, Michael’s experience spans a diverse range from the 19th and 20th Centuries. Extreme as Nat King Cole to Electronic Dance Music. Please be kind enough to listen and offer constructive criticism. This music was made for you, and with your thoughtful feedback, it will be improved for a larger audience.
